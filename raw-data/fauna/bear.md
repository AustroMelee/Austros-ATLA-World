```md
### 📖 Overview

The Bear is a rare, non-hybrid brown bear native to the Earth Kingdom's wilderness. Unlike the many hybrid creatures that populate the Avatar world, these pure bears are relatively uncommon and are often kept as pets by wealthy individuals. The most famous example is Bosco, the beloved pet of Earth King Kuei, who became a symbol of the Earth Kingdom's connection to nature.

### 🏞️ Habitat & Distribution

Bears are found in the remote wilderness areas of the Earth Kingdom, particularly in mountainous regions and dense forests. They prefer areas with plenty of cover, access to water sources, and abundant food supplies. Due to their rarity, they are often protected and carefully managed.

### 🎭 Notable Examples

- **Bosco**: The famous pet of Earth King Kuei, beloved throughout the Earth Kingdom
- **Forest Guardian**: A legendary bear that protects ancient groves
- **Mountain King**: A massive specimen that rules the highland territories

### 🏛️ Cultural Significance

In Earth Kingdom culture, the Bear represents purity and connection to the natural world. Their rarity and non-hybrid nature make them symbols of the original, unaltered beauty of nature. Bosco's relationship with the Earth King has made bears symbols of the monarchy's connection to the people and the land.

### 🐻 Behavior & Characteristics

Bears are generally solitary creatures, though they may form family groups during breeding season. They are most active during dawn and dusk, using their keen senses to locate food. They are intelligent and can form strong bonds with humans when raised in captivity.

### ⚡ Abilities

- **Enhanced Strength**: Possess incredible physical power
- **Keen Senses**: Excellent hearing and smell for detecting food and danger
- **Intelligence**: Highly intelligent and capable of learning
- **Adaptive Survival**: Can thrive in various environments

### 🍖 Diet

Bears are omnivorous, feeding on a wide variety of foods including berries, nuts, fish, small animals, and occasionally larger prey. They are excellent foragers and can adapt their diet to whatever is available in their environment.

### 📏 Size & Lifespan

- **Size**: Large, comparable to a full-grown brown bear
- **Weight**: 400-800 pounds
- **Lifespan**: 20-30 years in the wild

### 🛡️ Conservation Status

Bears are rare and protected throughout the Earth Kingdom. Their populations are carefully managed, and they are often kept in protected areas or as pets by wealthy individuals. They are considered a national treasure and are never hunted.
```
