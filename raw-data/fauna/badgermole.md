### 📖 Overview

The Badgermole is one of the most significant creatures in Avatar world history, being the original earthbender that taught humans the art of earthbending. These massive, blind subterranean mammals are revered throughout the Earth Kingdom for their connection to the ancient art of earthbending and their role in shaping the continent's underground networks.

### 🏞️ Habitat & Distribution

Badgermoles are found throughout the Earth Kingdom's vast underground networks, particularly in areas with rich earthbending history. They create extensive tunnel systems that can span hundreds of miles, connecting various regions of the Earth Kingdom beneath the surface.

### 🎭 Notable Examples

- **Ancient Teachers**: The original Badgermoles that taught humans earthbending
- **Toph's Masters**: The Badgermoles that trained Toph Beifong in earthbending
- **Tunnel Networkers**: Groups that maintain the vast underground passageways

### 🏛️ Cultural Significance

Badgermoles hold immense cultural and spiritual significance in the Earth Kingdom. They are considered the original earthbenders and are deeply respected by all earthbenders. Their ability to "see" through earthbending has made them symbols of perception beyond sight and the deep connection between living beings and the earth itself.

### 🦡 Behavior & Characteristics

Badgermoles are highly social creatures that live in complex family groups. Despite being blind, they navigate their underground world with perfect precision using earthbending. They are gentle giants, rarely aggressive unless their tunnels or young are threatened.

### ⚡ Abilities

- **Earthbending**: Master earthbenders that can manipulate earth, stone, and metal
- **Seismic Sense**: Can "see" through vibrations in the earth
- **Tunnel Creation**: Build extensive underground networks
- **Metalbending**: Some can bend metal, a rare and advanced skill

### 🍖 Diet

Badgermoles are omnivorous, feeding on roots, tubers, and small underground creatures. They use their earthbending abilities to locate and extract food from deep within the earth.

### 📏 Size & Lifespan

- **Size**: Massive, comparable to a small building
- **Weight**: Several tons
- **Lifespan**: 100+ years, some living for centuries

### 🛡️ Conservation Status

Badgermoles are protected and revered throughout the Earth Kingdom. Their populations are stable, though they prefer to remain hidden from human settlements. They are considered sacred animals and are never hunted or harmed.
