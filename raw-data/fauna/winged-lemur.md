Winged lemurs are highly intelligent and form strong bonds with their human companions.

- **Momo:** A well-known ring-tailed winged lemur who became a loyal companion to Team Avatar, particularly close to Aang and later Toph. Momo's playful antics and loyalty made him an essential member of the team.

### 🌍 Habitat & Distribution

Winged lemurs were commonly found in and around the Air Temples, where they lived in harmony with the Air Nomads. They prefer forested areas and mountainous regions where they can easily navigate through trees and cliffs using their flying abilities.

### 🔗 Cultural Significance

Winged lemurs represent the playful and free-spirited nature of the Air Nomads. Their ability to fly and their mischievous behavior embody the Air Nomad philosophy of freedom and joy. They serve as reminders of the importance of playfulness and curiosity in life.

### 🎭 Behavior & Characteristics

Winged lemurs are highly social and intelligent creatures. They are known for their playful behavior, curiosity, and ability to form strong bonds with humans. They are omnivorous, eating fruits, insects, and small animals. Their large ears provide excellent hearing, and their bat-like wings allow for agile flight and gliding.
