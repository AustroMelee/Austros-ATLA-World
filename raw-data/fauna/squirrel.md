## 📖 UI - EXPANDED VIEW

```md
### 📖 Overview

The Squirrel is a small, non-hybrid rodent found at the Eastern Air Temple. These common creatures are known for their agility, bushy tails, and ability to climb trees and navigate the temple's complex architecture. Squirrels are one of the few non-hybrid animals in the Avatar world, representing a connection to the natural world as we know it.

### ✨ Notable Examples

- **Eastern Temple Squirrels:** Various squirrels that inhabit the Eastern Air Temple, using the temple's structures and surrounding trees for shelter and food storage.

### 🌍 Habitat & Distribution

Squirrels are commonly found in forested areas and around the Air Temples, particularly the Eastern Air Temple where they can find abundant food sources and suitable nesting locations. They prefer areas with trees and structures where they can build nests and store food.

### 🔗 Cultural Significance

Squirrels represent the connection between the Avatar world and our own natural world. As one of the few non-hybrid animals, they serve as a reminder of the familiar aspects of nature that exist alongside the fantastical hybrid creatures of the Avatar universe.

### 🎭 Behavior & Characteristics

Squirrels are highly agile and intelligent rodents known for their ability to climb trees and navigate complex environments. They are omnivorous, eating nuts, seeds, fruits, and occasionally small insects. They are known for their food-hoarding behavior and excellent memory for locating stored food.
```
