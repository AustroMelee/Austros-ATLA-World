### 📖 Overview

The Spider Bat is a hybrid creature combining the characteristics of a spider and a bat. These unusual creatures inhabited the abandoned Eastern Air Temple after the Air Nomad Genocide, making their home in the deserted temple structures. Spider bats are known for their web-spinning abilities combined with flight capabilities, creating a unique adaptation to their environment.

### ✨ Notable Examples

- **Temple Inhabitants:** Various spider bats that made their home in the Eastern Air Temple, creating webs throughout the abandoned structure.

### 🌍 Habitat & Distribution

Spider bats are primarily found in abandoned structures and caves, particularly in the Eastern Air Temple where they established colonies after the Air Nomad Genocide. They prefer dark, enclosed spaces where they can build their webs and roost safely.

### 🔗 Cultural Significance

Spider bats represent the transformation of sacred spaces after tragedy. Their presence in the abandoned Air Temple symbolizes how nature reclaims spaces left behind by civilization, while also serving as a reminder of the devastation caused by the Air Nomad Genocide.

### 🎭 Behavior & Characteristics

Spider bats combine the web-spinning abilities of spiders with the flight capabilities of bats. They are nocturnal creatures that build webs for catching prey and roosting. They are generally solitary or live in small groups, and are adapted to living in dark, enclosed environments.
