### 📖 Overview

The Dragon Moose is a hybrid creature native to the Fire Nation, combining characteristics of dragons and moose. These creatures are primarily used for pulling carriages and other heavy loads, making them valuable for transportation and commerce throughout the Fire Nation.

### 🏔️ Habitat & Distribution

Dragon Moose are found throughout the Fire Nation, particularly in areas where they are bred and trained for transportation work. They are well-adapted to the nation's volcanic environment and can navigate the challenging terrain that characterizes much of the Fire Nation's landscape.

### 🎭 Cultural Significance

In Fire Nation society, Dragon Moose represent the nation's practical approach to transportation and their ability to adapt creatures for specific purposes. They are commonly used by merchants, nobles, and military personnel for their strength and reliability in pulling heavy loads.

### 🐾 Behavior & Abilities

Dragon Moose are known for their strength and endurance, making them ideal for pulling carriages and heavy loads. They are generally docile when properly trained and form strong bonds with their handlers. Their hybrid nature gives them the strength of a moose combined with the resilience of dragon heritage.

### 🍖 Diet & Hunting

These creatures are herbivorous, feeding primarily on vegetation and plant matter. In domestic settings, they are fed a carefully managed diet that maintains their strength and energy levels for transportation work while keeping them healthy and manageable.

### 📏 Size & Physical Characteristics

Dragon Moose are large creatures with powerful builds designed for pulling heavy loads. Their size and strength make them excellent for transportation work, while their hybrid nature provides them with the resilience needed to work in the Fire Nation's challenging environment.

### ⏰ Lifespan & Reproduction

These hybrids have moderate lifespans and are bred specifically for transportation work. The Fire Nation maintains breeding programs to ensure a steady supply of these creatures for commercial and military use.

### 🛡️ Conservation Status

Due to their value as transportation animals, Dragon Moose are actively bred and maintained throughout the Fire Nation. They are not considered endangered and are readily available for purchase or rental by those who need transportation services.
