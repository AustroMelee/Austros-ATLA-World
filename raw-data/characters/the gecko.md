### 🧩 Role in the Story

The Gecko was a contestant in the underground earthbending competition Earth Rumble. He wore a distinct bright-green mask and used unique tactics during battle, such as crawling on all fours and using his earthbending to scale walls. He relied on his quick speed and agility to climb around the ring and stay out of the reach of his opponents. The Gecko was defeated by The Boulder and later by Toph Beifong during her rescue of Aang. He also helped Xin Fu attempt to kidnap Aang and Toph.
