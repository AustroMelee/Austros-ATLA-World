### 📖 Overview

Yu is a renowned earthbending master and head of Gaoling’s earthbending academy. Known for his teaching, subtle earthbending style, and profit-driven approach, Yu was hired to retrieve Toph Beifong but ultimately returned to his teaching profession.

### 🧩 Role in the Story

Yu was an earthbending master and a resident of the upper-class Earth Kingdom town of Gaoling. As manager and primary instructor of the town's earthbending academy, he was more interested in making money than passing on an art form. He later became a bounty hunter, searching for Toph upon her father's request, but returned to his teaching profession after failing to capture his target. Yu’s subtle, conservative earthbending style contrasted with Xin Fu’s brute force, and he was known for his patience, easygoing nature, and profit-driven mindset.
