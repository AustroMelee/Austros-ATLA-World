### 📖 Overview

The Big Bad Hippo is a massive earthbending wrestler from Gaoling, known for his unique fighting style, third-person speech, and later, his role as a soldier in the invasion of the Fire Nation. He is recognized for his strength, resilience, and signature move, "rocking the boat."

### 🧩 Role in the Story

Initially an earthbending wrestler in Earth Rumble, he later became a soldier for his country when he, along with The Boulder, participated in the invasion of the Fire Nation. He was one of the most recognizable fighters in the Earth Rumble tournaments, due to his extremely large size and unique way of communicating. He was later captured during the failed invasion but freed after the war, attending Zuko's coronation with Toph on his shoulders.
